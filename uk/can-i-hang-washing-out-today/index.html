FAQs

What matters most for drying washing outside?

Rain risk, daylight, humidity, temperature and wind. A cool humid day can leave washing damp even if it does not rain.

Is evening good for drying washing?

Usually not. Drying slows as temperature falls and humidity rises, so the checker prefers earlier daylight windows.
