Should I wash my car today?

Check your local conditions

Quick answer

A good car-washing day is dry, not too cold, not too windy and unlikely to rain immediately after you finish.

GO when...

  • Rain risk is low for the next few hours.
  • Temperatures are safely above freezing.
  • Wind is not strong enough to make rinsing difficult.
  • There is enough daylight to finish properly.

CAUTION when...

  • Light showers are possible later.
  • It is cold enough for water to sit on the car for longer.
  • Wind may blow dirt or debris around.
  • Roads may still be wet or muddy.

NO-GO when...

  • Rain is likely soon.
  • It is near freezing or icy.
  • Strong wind could make washing unpleasant.
  • Storms or heavy showers are expected.

FAQs

Is it worth washing my car before rain?

Usually not if rain is expected soon, especially if roads are dirty or wet.

Can I wash my car when it is cold?

Avoid washing when temperatures are near freezing because water can freeze on surfaces or the ground.

Is wind a problem when washing a car?

Strong wind can blow dirt, leaves or debris onto the car while you are washing it.

Should I wash my car in direct sun?

Very strong sun can dry soap too quickly, so mild dry conditions are usually better.

Can I check tomorrow instead?

Yes, choose tomorrow or weekend on the checker for a better washing window.
