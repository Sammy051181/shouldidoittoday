FAQs

Can I cut wet grass?

It is usually better to wait. Wet grass clumps, tears rather than cuts cleanly, and can leave the lawn and mower in a mess.

How long after rain should I wait?

As a rule of thumb, wait until the grass blades and ground surface are dry. The checker looks at recent rain and the next useful dry window.
