Should I mow the lawn today?

Get a local GO, CAUTION or NO-GO for mowing based on rain, wind, temperature and recent weather.

Check your local conditions

Short answer

Mow when the lawn is dry enough, rain is unlikely for the next few hours, wind is manageable and the temperature is safe for outdoor work.

Why the weather matters

Mowing is a weather decision. Wet grass, storms, strong wind, heat and cold can all turn a normal job into a bad idea.

Check my local grass weather

GO when...

  • Grass is dry enough to cut cleanly.
  • Rain risk is low.
  • Wind is manageable.
  • Temperature is comfortable enough for mowing.

CAUTION when...

  • Recent rain may have left the lawn damp.
  • Showers are possible.
  • It is hot, cold or windy enough to check conditions.
  • The lawn may be soft or stressed.

NO-GO when...

  • It is raining or storms are likely.
  • The lawn is wet, muddy or slippery.
  • It is too hot or too cold.
  • Strong wind or lightning risk exists.

FAQs

Can I mow wet grass?

It is better to wait. Wet grass can clog the mower and leave clumps.

Should I mow before rain?

Only if the lawn is dry and you have enough time before rain arrives.

How soon after rain can I mow?

Wait until the grass and soil are dry enough to avoid clumps and tracks.

What is the best time to mow today?

Usually late morning or afternoon once dew has dried and before evening damp returns.

Can I check tomorrow instead?

Yes, use the checker to compare today, tomorrow and the weekend.
